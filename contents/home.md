💻 **Research Vision**

My work is driven by a central question:  
**how can embodied agents learn, simulate, and adapt to the physical world efficiently and at scale?**

I view **world models as system-level constructs**, where intelligence emerges from the interaction between  
**sequence modeling, memory, and computation under constraints**.

---

🧠 **Research Direction**

I focus on **world modeling and continual adaptation** in embodied systems, targeting core bottlenecks in:

- **long-horizon reasoning**  
- **data efficiency**  
- **real-time deployment under physical constraints**

My approach is fundamentally **system-driven**.  
I explore **system–algorithm co-design**, treating model architectures and hardware efficiency as a unified design space.
